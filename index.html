At Credebt we have been supporting the invoice finance Industry for 11 years and during this time have recovered over £350 million for funders when a collect out is required as well as helping many Funder’s clients improve their Collections.

With over 3000 Debtor Collateral reviews carried out during this time, which are always free of charges to funders, we have uncovered issues that Audits and even IBR’s have not.

Once we review the client we report and suggest the best way forward always with the Funder’s capital as our number 1 Priority!

We are widely considered Risk Management Specialists and are regularly engages by banks and independents funders alike whenever they have concerns about a client.

This can include the 90 day column being too high, high levels of credit notes or invoice queries, poor verification results or any other concerns with a client in your portfolio.

Credebt are discreet yet effective and we are recommended by many funders. Visit www.credebt.co.uk to see what our clients say about us.
